Fruits of Diversity Community Orchard

Is located at 4375 N Trenton, or about 200 yards north of the intersection of N Alaska and N Trenton (Bus Stop ID: 5862). Walk north along the sidewalk adjacent to Village Gardens' Seeds of Harmony Community Garden to reach the orchard.
